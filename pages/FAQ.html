Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Q: What are your cafe's opening hours?

A: We are open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 am to 10:00 pm. On weekends, we open at 9:00 am and close at 11:00 pm.

Q: Where is your cafe located?

A: We are located at 123 Main Street, Cityville, State, Zip Code. You can find detailed directions on our Contact page.

Q: Do you offer outdoor seating?

A: Yes, we have a beautiful outdoor patio where you can enjoy your meals and drinks.

Menu

Q: What types of food do you serve?

A: We offer a diverse menu with options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Our menu includes sandwiches, salads, pastries, coffee, tea, and more. Visit our Menu page to explore the full range of options.

Q: Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

A: Absolutely! We have a selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes available on our menu. Our staff is also happy to accommodate any dietary restrictions or allergies.

Q: Can I make special requests for my order?

A: Yes, we do our best to accommodate special requests. Please let our staff know about any specific dietary requirements or preferences, and we will strive to meet your needs.
